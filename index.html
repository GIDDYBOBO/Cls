Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom furniture take to complete?
Typically, custom furniture projects take 4-8 weeks from design approval to delivery, depending on the complexity and size of the order. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.
Do you offer warranties on your furniture?
Yes, we provide a comprehensive 2-year warranty on all craftsmanship and materials. This covers any manufacturing defects and ensures your investment is protected.
Can you work within my budget?
Absolutely! We offer furniture solutions across various price points. During consultation, we'll discuss your budget and recommend materials and designs that deliver the best value.
Do you provide free consultations and quotes?
Yes, we offer complimentary initial consultations and detailed quotes. This includes space assessment, design recommendations, and transparent pricing with no hidden costs.
What types of materials do you work with?
We work with a wide range of premium materials including solid wood, engineered wood, metal, glass, and various upholstery fabrics. We source materials from trusted suppliers to ensure quality and sustainability.
